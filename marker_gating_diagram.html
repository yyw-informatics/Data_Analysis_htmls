cell_type_flow mapping
  • ILC cells are reclassified into flow-comparable categories (Lin−CD127+ → CRTH2/CD117 quadrant)
  • flow_ILC2 = CRTH2+
  • flow_ILC3 = CRTH2− CD117+
  • flow_ILC1 = CRTH2− CD117−
  • ILCP_type3_biased maps to flow_ILC3 (if from CD117+ ILCP) or flow_ILC1 (if from CD117− ILCP_KITlow)
  • NK, pDC, and other populations retain their coarse label in cell_type_flow
Two-step MACS enrichment workflow
  • Step 1: anti-CD56 positive selection captures NK cells (~30% of final pool)
  • Step 2: Lineage-PE depletion (anti-CD3/CD11c/CD14/CD19) enriches ILCs from the CD56-negative flow-through (~70%)
  • Fractions recombined ~30:70 before 10x Chromium loading
  • No CD123 in depletion panel — pDCs survive, explaining the large pDC_like population (23%)
  • CD127/IL7R ADT clone is mouse-reactive and non-functional on human cells
